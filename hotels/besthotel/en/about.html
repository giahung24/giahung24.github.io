About the Hotel

A comfortable and accessible hotel south of Bordeaux

Looking for a comfortable and accessible hotel in the Bordeaux area?

Welcome to Best Hotel Bordeaux Sud, our friendly 43-room 2-star property designed for both business trips and family getaways. Ideally located close to the A630 ring road, our hotel gives you quick access to Bordeaux city centre, its historic districts, and the region's main routes. Whether you are here for work, a weekend break, or a holiday, our location is a real asset for exploring the region with ease.

An ideal location for your travels

Our hotel is only a few minutes from the University of Bordeaux and the business areas in the south of the city. You can also easily enjoy:

  • the quays of the Port of the Moon, a UNESCO World Heritage site
  • green spaces such as Château de Thouars Park
  • or Parc Peixotto for relaxing moments

Our location is ideal for combining work, leisure, and discovering Bordeaux.

Rooms designed for your comfort

Whether you are traveling alone, as a couple, or with family, enjoy comfortable and functional rooms adapted to your needs. Our rooms feature:

  • Carefully selected comfortable bedding
  • Air conditioning for year-round comfort
  • Private bathroom
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Flat-screen TV with a variety of channels

Several room types are available: double, twin, or family rooms to suit every stay. Accessible rooms for guests with reduced mobility are also available.

A full breakfast to start the day right

Every morning, enjoy a generous and varied breakfast buffet to begin your day. On the menu:

  • Fresh pastries and breads
  • Sweet and savory items
  • Hot drinks (coffee, tea, hot chocolate)
  • Fruit juices and dairy products

Whether you prefer a hearty, light, or savory breakfast, there is something for everyone.

Services designed for your stay

To make your stay even more enjoyable, we provide:

  • Free private parking
  • Electric vehicle charging station
  • Unlimited Wi-Fi access
  • Practical common areas
  • Microwave available for guests

Pets are also welcome for an additional fee, so the whole family can enjoy the stay.

Discover Bordeaux and the surrounding area

From Best Hotel Bordeaux Sud, explore the cultural and heritage treasures of Bordeaux:

  • Its historic city centre
  • Its many restaurants and terraces
  • Its parks and natural areas
  • Its vineyards known around the world

Whether you are here with family, friends, or for business, Bordeaux offers many activities within easy reach of the hotel.
