About Us

Welcome to Best Hôtel Bordeaux Sud, your ideal home base in Villenave-d'Ornon, located just minutes from the vibrant heart of Bordeaux.

Our 2-star establishment offers a warm welcome with a 24/7 reception, making it the perfect choice for both business trips and tourist getaways in the Gironde region. We provide modern, air-conditioned, and fully soundproofed rooms designed to ensure maximum comfort and a restful night's sleep.

To make your stay as convenient as possible, we offer a free and secure private car park (equipped with electric vehicle charging stations), high-speed Wi-Fi, and a delicious buffet breakfast served every morning. On sunny days, you can relax on our terrace or in the garden. Fully accessible to guests with reduced mobility and happy to welcome your pets, our hotel combines comfort, simplicity, and a friendly atmosphere to make you feel right at home.

Ideally located, our establishment is just a stone's throw from exit 17 of the Bordeaux ring road. This prime location guarantees quick and easy access, allowing you to reach Bordeaux city center or the region's main transport links in no time.
