About Us

Located in Villenave-d'Ornon, just 8km from Bordeaux, Best Hotel Bordeaux Sud offers a comfortable 2-star stay with a garden and terrace. Our establishment provides a 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi and an airport shuttle service for your convenience.

Enjoy air-conditioned rooms equipped with Canal+ and modern amenities. Start your day with our highly-rated buffet breakfast before exploring the beautiful Bordeaux region.
